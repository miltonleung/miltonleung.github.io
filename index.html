Guess Again!

an iOS game that takes guessing games like Pictionary and Charades to the next level
developed and designed in September 2016
VIEW ON GITHUB DOWNLOAD APP

Guessing games are the key to a great dinner party and Guess Again is here to impress your guests and have lots of fun! With preloaded lists of over 500 entries to draw from, and the ability to create your own public or private lists, the entertainment is endless. Some lists include Billboard Top 100 Artists, Vulture's Most Valuable Stars of 2015, Hollywood Reporters's Best TV Shows Ever, All Time Top 660 Grossing Movies.

Rocket Poll

an iOS app where users can ask questions and express opinions by asking and voting
developed and designed with UBC Launchpad in September 2016
VIEW ON GITHUB DOWNLOAD APP

From asking friends to collecting answers from a lecture of 300 students, Rocket Poll makes this process fast and easy. With a simple interface, you can start asking in 10 seconds or less. Ask week long questions with page long answers, and even see who said no to your party this weekend!

Small Steps and Little Victories

an iOS app that quantifies exercise data into fun and interesting milestones
developed and designed in June 2016
VIEW ON GITHUB DOWNLOAD APP

Troubled by the enormous amounts of health data thrown at us by most fitness trackers, I set out to solve this problem with my first solo attempt at an iOS application. I created milestones to quantify the kilometers and steps we have been chasing after as well as a smart progress tracker to change our goals according to our performance.

B Prime Medical

a website where potential cusomers can contact and seek more information about B Prime Medical, a biomedical practice
developed and designed in April 2016
VIEW ON GITHUB VIEW WEBSITE

When rebuilding the website for B Prime Medical, ease of use and reducing the amount of time to find information was the highest priority. I decided to consolidate the five existing pages into a simple, yet clear presentation of the practice. Through this project, I learned more about utilizing Bootstrap for quick deployment and making custom UI elements with Sketch.
