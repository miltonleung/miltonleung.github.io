I made this

Small Steps and Little Victories

an iOS app that quantifies exercise data into fun and interesting milestones
developed and designed in June 2016
VIEW ON GITHUB DOWNLOAD APP

Troubled by the enormous amounts of health data thrown at us by most fitness trackers, I set out to solve this problem with my first solo attempt at an iOS application. I created milestones to quantify the kilometers and steps we have been chasing after as well as a smart progress tracker to change our goals according to our performance.
